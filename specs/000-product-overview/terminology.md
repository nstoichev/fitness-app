# Terminology

- Workout: A complete training session scheduled on a calendar date.
- Segment: A logical block within a workout (e.g. AMRAP, Sets, Fartlek).
- Exercise: A physical movement (e.g. Deadlift, Push-up).
- Format: The structural rule of a segment (AMRAP, EMOM, For Time, etc).
- Performance: User-entered execution data (time, weight).
- Timer: A generated execution clock derived from segment definitions.
- Stamina: A muscle-group-based recovery indicator.
- Fatigue: Accumulated training load applied to a muscle group.
